About Me

I am a fourth year PhD student in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) working with Vera Mikyoung Hur. Prior to coming to the UIUC, I attended the National Institute of Science, Education and Reserach in India, where I obtained my Integrated M.Sc. in mathematics with a minor in physics.

Curriculum Vitae

Research

My research interest lies in the area of nonlinear PDEs. For my PhD thesis, I am studying instabilities of some nonlinear dispersive PDEs associated with water waves such as Benjamin-Bona-Mahony (BBM) equation, Boussinesq equations, Kadomtsev-Petviashvili (KP) equation, Camasa-Holm (CH) equation etc.
